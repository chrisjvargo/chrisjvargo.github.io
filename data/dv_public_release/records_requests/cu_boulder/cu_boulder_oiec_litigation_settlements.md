Access regime to verify before sending: CORA and University of Colorado records procedures; FERPA and privacy redactions expected.

Request anonymized, machine-readable records from 2000-01-01 through present concerning CU Boulder OIEC, Title IX, student-conduct, faculty, employment, and related administrative allegations involving sexual misconduct, intimate-partner violence, harassment, stalking, protection orders, false-reporting allegations, or respondent claims of deficient process.

Requested fields:

- anonymous matter/respondent/complainant keys;
- recorded or self-identified sex/gender where collected;
- allegation category;
- policy version and standard of proof;
- dates of report, investigation, hearing, decision, appeal, sanction, reversal, settlement, and litigation;
- outcome, appeal outcome, finding of no violation, reversal reason, process-error finding, sex-discrimination finding, sanction, transcript notation, suspension/expulsion/employment/housing consequences, and relief;
- litigation case numbers, settlement agreements, payment records, regent/authorization materials, and insurer/risk records;
- records-retention and deletion schedules.

Request specific retrieval for the reported 2015 anonymous male-student settlement and the 2016 Doe v. DiStefano matter, including executed settlement, dismissal papers, final disposition, and payment records.
