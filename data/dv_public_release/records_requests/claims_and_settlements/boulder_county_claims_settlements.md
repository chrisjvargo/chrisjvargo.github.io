# Boulder County Claims And Settlements Request

Access regime to verify before sending: CORA for claims, settlements, payment, risk-management, insurance, commissioner materials, and administrative records; CCJRA where criminal-justice records are embedded.

Request claims registers, notices, lawsuits, settlements, judgments, verdicts, fee awards, insurer payments, indemnification records, and outside-counsel invoices from 2000-01-01 to present involving Boulder County Sheriff's Office, jail, prosecutors where county-held, or county officials in domestic-violence, sexual-assault, protection-order, harassment, malicious-prosecution, false-arrest, evidence-fabrication, disclosure, or failure-to-investigate matters.

Request records sufficient to identify party role, recorded sex/gender where available, allegation category, agency, dates, disposition, payment amount, payment source, and no-admission clauses without unnecessary identifiers.
