# Boulder County Sheriff's Office Records Request

Custodian: Boulder County Sheriff's Office.

Access regime to verify before sending: CCJRA for criminal-justice records; CORA for policy, training, contract, and system records.

Request deidentified structured CAD/RMS/enforcement/referral records for unincorporated Boulder County and contract service to Lyons, Nederland, and Superior. Keep geographic jurisdiction, responding agency/unit, arresting agency, dispatching agency, and referring agency separate.

Request call-code dictionaries, incident/offense codes, disposition definitions, policy manuals, domestic-violence and predominant-aggressor policy versions, body-camera policy versions, training materials, contract-service dates, staffing/beat identifiers, and data migration logs.

Request no-arrest, report-only, civil-standby, custody-exchange, property-retrieval, protection-order, harassment, disturbance, welfare-check, assault, and domestic-code contacts, not only arrests. Request metadata for narratives/media first, then a stratified sample.
