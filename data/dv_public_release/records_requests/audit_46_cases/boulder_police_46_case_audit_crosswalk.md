# Boulder Police 46-Case Audit Crosswalk Request

Access regime to verify before sending: CCJRA for case-level criminal-justice records; CORA for policy, audit, supervision, discipline, and administrative records.

Request a deidentified case-level crosswalk for the 46 matters reviewed in the Twentieth Judicial District Attorney's May 25, 2023 independent audit of cases assigned to Detective Kwame Williams from 2019 through 2022.

Requested fields:

- anonymous case/event/person keys;
- incident date, report date, assignment date, investigation start, closure date, referral date, filing/no-file date, and final disposition;
- allegation category and relationship category;
- recorded sex or gender of involved parties;
- whether an arrest, warrant, summons, referral, or filing occurred before investigation completion;
- whether additional investigation strengthened, weakened, or did not affect probable cause;
- exact no-file, insufficient-evidence, or statute-of-limitations reason;
- statute-of-limitations status;
- investigative actions omitted and later completed;
- complaint, civil claim, settlement, discipline, or oversight follow-up indicator;
- policy/supervisor review timestamps.

Request code tables, data dictionary, redaction rules, and lower-cost fallback of aggregate tables by allegation category, sex/gender, year, and outcome.
