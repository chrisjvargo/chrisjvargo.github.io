# DV Claim Style Guide

Use stage-specific language. Do not collapse arrest, referral, filing, conviction, reversal, settlement, and adjudicated damages.

Permitted lead language for the current release:

> The available public release cannot verify the asserted final gender-disparity findings.

Do not use final disparity headlines until the hypothesis-verification table shows support under the preregistered rules.

The build language linter rejects inflammatory phrases and legal conclusions without required proof context.
